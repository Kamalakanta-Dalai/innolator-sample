    WELCOME TO INNOLATOR:

    Escalating Innovations...

    WHERE YOUR INNOVATIONS REACH OUT TO THE WORLD...

    If you think you have an innovation which can bring about even a small change in any person’s life on this planet then you are definitely for us !! Submit your idea today and win exciting rewards and recognition.

    IDEAS AND INNOVATIONS

    is the practical implementation of ideas that result in the introduction of new goods or services or improvement in offering goods or services.

    Imagine you saw your mom struggling with the daily household chores of brooming and mopping the floor of your house and you came out with a solution to her problem : you innovated a machine called “Vacuum cleaners”. Well, vacuum cleaners are just one example out of the millions of innovations we are surrounded by and they need not be as big as the vacuum cleaner. From tooth brushes to safety pins — all are useful innovations which made our life easier. Innovations may not always be new gadgets . They can also be a better model of the existing one like the wireless mouse is better version of the wired one. They can also be the cheaper version of something built out of scrap like roads built out of plastics. And when it comes to innovations who else can be better than Indians! We, at INNOLATOR are on a mission to identify Indian ” Jugaads ” (ideas/ innovations) which not only make our lives easier but are also cost effective and pave way for a sustainable tomorrow.

    WHAT'S YOUR IDEAS...

    Your ideas can belong to any field from science and engineering to management and banking. Some of the fields are as follows :

    1. Science
    2. Engineering
    3. Medicine
    4. Lifestyle And Fashion
    5. Banking And Finance
    6. Business And Management Ideas
    7. Teaching Methods
    8. Home Science And Hotel Management
    9. Any Other...

    ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA....

    • Anyone who thinks has an idea/innovation/solution to existing problems /problems you want a solution to can participate here.
    • This contest is open to people of all age groups and professions.
    • You do not need any educational qualifications/degrees to participate here.
    • Citizens of India are eligible to participate.
    • To know more about the kind of ideas that you can submit , scroll down and read the IDEAS section.

    RULES AND REGULATIONS....

    • Do not judge your own idea. Even if people/friends/relatives say that it is a silly one and make fun of you saying it is Indian “Jugaad” then also submit your idea here.
    • Once you submit your idea here , kindly wait patiently and do not submit the same idea anywhere else.If done so, then you will be disqualified from the contest.
    • Every participant can submit as many ideas as he/she likes.
    • You can submit both- solutions to problems and problems (faced by large group of people) to which our team will find out solutions.

    REWARDS AND RECOGNITION.....

    • If one of your ideas gets selected then you will get :
    • A certificate of merit from Innolator delivered to you by post or to your e-mail address ( as per your convinience).
    • Monetary help to turn your idea into reality.
    • Assistance from an expert team of leading professionals.
    • A patent filed (free of cost).
    • A chance to get internship offers (Both paid or non-paid).

    HOW TO SUBMIT YOUR IDEA

    • Click on the SUBMIT button present on the top right corner of the Home page.
    • A Registration page appears. Fill all the required fields and click on the submit button.

    WHAT HAPPENS AFTER YOU SUBMIT YOUR IDEA...?

    • After you submit your idea, our group of experts consisting of top professionals from every field verify and analyze your idea.
    • We get back to you within 2 working days to hold a small conversation.
    • A three step verification process takes place and when approved ,the idea is sent to the Reward allotment team .
    • The rewards allotment team decides the suitable rewards .
    • Side by side, the finance team decides the budget for patent and technical assistance.
    • The Technical team analyses the requirements of your ideas and prepares a blue print.
    • Your idea is then ready to be presented to the world !!
